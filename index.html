What is artificial intelligence (AI)?

There are lots of different definitions of AI, but we define it as: hardware or software that exhibits intelligent behaviour. By intelligent behaviour, we mean the ability to calculate, understand, reason, plan, communicate, learn and perceive. Theres no one type of artificial intelligence and it can be used for all sorts of different purposes.

Why is AI important?

AI is helping people to achieve things that we couldnt otherwise do. It cant do many of the things that the human brain is capable of, or the variety, but it can do certain specialised things very well. For example, AI is much better at identifying patterns and trends in large data sets than people are, making it a potential powerful tool for businesses.

What can AI do for my business?

There are all sorts of applications for AI in business, from product development, to sales and marketing, to warehousing, logistics and supply chain monitoring. Among other things, it can be used to identify different groups among customers for targeting, for forecasting the demand of products and services, for identifying risks in a companys supply chain well in advance and for improving the quality of lead generation and scoring.

What data can I use with AI?

AI can work with any type of data. In addition to typical business fare such as sales figures, item inventories and logistics information, it can also work with unstructured data like emails and social media interactions. For businesses though, the key is to understand how AI might help you achieve some aspect of your business strategy and to identify what data it would need to do that.
